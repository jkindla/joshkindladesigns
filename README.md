## 🔧 Customization Guide

### 1. **Update Your Contact Information**
   - Find the email address in the contact section: `josh@joshkindladesigns.com`
   - Replace with your actual email

### 2. **Add Your Portfolio Images**
   - Replace the placeholder images (via.placeholder.com URLs) with your actual project screenshots
   - Update the portfolio item titles and descriptions to match your real projects
   - Example projects already included:
     - Restaurant websites
     - Artist portfolios
     - E-commerce shops
     - Service business sites

### 3. **Update Your Color Scheme**
   - In `styles.css`, find the `:root` section at the top
   - Customize these colors to match your brand:
     ```css
     --primary-color: #2563eb;      /* Main blue */
     --secondary-color: #1e40af;    /* Darker blue */
     --accent-color: #f59e0b;       /* Orange */
     --dark-color: #1f2937;         /* Dark gray */
     ```

### 4. **Modify Your Services**
   - Add or remove service cards in the services section
   - Update the emoji icons and descriptions to match your offerings

### 5. **Adjust Pricing**
   - Update the pricing amounts to match your rates
   - Add or remove pricing tiers as needed
   - Modify the features list for each tier

### 6. **Update Hero Text**
   - Change the main headline and tagline to your own words
   - This is in the `.hero` section
